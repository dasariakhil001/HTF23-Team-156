Butter Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken, boneless
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, chili powder, turmeric, and salt.
  2. Heat butter, add onions, ginger-garlic paste, and saut√©.
  3. Add tomato puree, spices, and cook.
  4. Add marinated chicken, cook till done, and add cream before serving.
