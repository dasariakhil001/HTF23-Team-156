Samosa Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • Potato filling (potatoes, peas, spices)

Instructions:

  1. Mix flour and oil, add water to make a stiff dough.
  2. Divide into small balls and roll each into a thin oval.
  3. Cut in half.
  4. Form a cone with each half, fill with potato mixture, and seal the edges.
  5. Deep fry until golden brown.
