Butter Chicken

  • 500g chicken, boneless
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • Salt to taste

Instructions: Marinate chicken in yogurt, chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Heat butter, add onions, ginger-garlic paste, and sauté. Add tomato puree, spices, and cook. Add marinated chicken, cook till done, and add cream before serving.

Chana Masala

  • 2 cups chickpeas, cooked
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Instructions: Heat oil, add cumin seeds, onions, and ginger-garlic paste. Sauté until golden brown. Add tomato puree, spices, and cook. Add chickpeas, simmer, and garnish with coriander leaves before serving.

Vegetable Pulao

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • Assorted vegetables (carrots, peas, potatoes)
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup fried onions for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Instructions: Cook rice. Sauté onions, add ginger-garlic paste, and vegetables. Add spices, yogurt, and cook. Layer rice and vegetable mix, garnish with fried onions and cilantro. Cover and cook on low heat.

Aloo Gobi

  • 2 potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Instructions: Heat oil, add cumin seeds, onions, and ginger-garlic paste. Sauté until golden brown. Add tomatoes, spices, and cook until oil separates. Add potatoes, cauliflower, and cook until tender. Garnish with coriander leaves before serving.
