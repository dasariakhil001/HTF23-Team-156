Palak Paneer Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 2 cups spinach, blanched and pureed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, pureed
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Saut√© onions, add ginger-garlic paste, and tomato puree.
  2. Add spinach puree, spices, and cook.
  3. Add paneer and cream, simmer until the gravy thickens.
  4. Serve hot.
