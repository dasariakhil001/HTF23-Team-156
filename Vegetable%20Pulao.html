Vegetable Pulao Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • Assorted vegetables (carrots, peas, potatoes)
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup fried onions for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook rice.
  2. Saut√© onions, add ginger-garlic paste, and vegetables.
  3. Add spices, yogurt, and cook.
  4. Layer rice and vegetable mix, garnish with fried onions and cilantro.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat.
